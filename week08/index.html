Transforms

  • Transforms can be used to manipulate many properties of elements
  • You can mess with the X and Y axis
  • Translate: Move
  • Scale
  • Rotate

Transitions

  • Transitions change a certain property over a certain amount of time
  • transition: width 2s;
  • Provide the poerty to be changed followed by a time
  • Keyframes are also a way of doing transitions while being more specific
