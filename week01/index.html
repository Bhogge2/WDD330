Importance of Mobile First

Many people today are still trying to design for computer first. This shouldn't be the case. Just look at how often you see people browsing their phones. Many people will see your site in a web format, so we must be sure that experience is smooth. When we design for mobile, we also need to be sure that the weight of the pages is low for those on a 3G connection.
